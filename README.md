# bank management system 
<b> Intoduction :  </b> Bank is a financial institution, which takes money in the form of deposits from the account holders and 
pay them specific intrest on their deposits after some period of time. 
bank disbursed these deposited money then used to provide loans for some period of time with a specift interest rate.
bank's revenue come from the difference of interest paid to the account holders and recieved interset from the given loan.

Banks are very useful institution which plays a crucial role in development of nation and individuals by providing funds and manging assests.
In india banks are strictly regulated by Reserve Bank of India in short RBI,
RBI is the national bank of india, independly works under name of government of india.
RBI also known as bank of all the indian banks.

What is Bank Management system ?
Bank Management is a system which controls all the operations of a bank like assest management, loan disbursedment and exchange of money.
A bank has many department to ensure sustainable working of the insitution and security.
